# IT'S NOT PIRACY!!!

## Free Stuff
If the content was originally free (e.g. PictoChat, Swapnote, GBA BIOS, etc.) and you're using it for your own personal (non-commercial) use, then **it's not piracy**. Missed the chance to get _Flipnote Studio 3D_? Don't have any modded hardware to dump the GBA BIOS? Just download it! It's not piracy if it's always been free.

## Stuff You Own
As long as you don't sell them or anything, it's okay to download ROMs of games you already own. If you have a modded Wii and want to dump your games, then it's pretty easy. But what if you have a N64 game pak and don't want to buy all the hardware you need for dumping it? Just download the ROM! If you legitimately own the game, them **it's not piracy**.

## Homebrew Community
Why do you call this piracy?! It's very clearly not piracy! Sure, it doesn't come from an official source, but you **already own the game legitimately**. So stop calling it piracy! The whole point of anti-piracy stuff is that piracy harms developers. If you already bought the game (or the content is free), then it doesn't harm the developers at all.
